RestRadar Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 11, 2023

Thank you for using RestRadar. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard the data you provide when using RestRadar on various devices and platforms, including iPhone, Mac, iPad, and others.

Data Collection

We prioritize your data privacy and adhere to strict data collection practices. We do not collect any personally identifiable information about you. Our data collection is limited to essential information required for debugging purposes and analyzing download and purchase trends.

Information Collected by RestRadar

RestRadar collects anonymized metadata utilizing Apple's built-in metrics system. This system captures data such as session length, crash reports, device type, and locale. Additionally, we employ RevenueCat for anonymized purchase tracking. For more information about RevenueCat's privacy policy, please refer to their official documentation.

We also utilize AdMob to display advertisements. To understand how AdMob handles data, please review their privacy policy.

Furthermore, we use Telemetry Deck for data analytics. To learn about Telemetry Deck's privacy practices, please refer to their privacy policy.

No other information is collected at any time. Any data you input into RestRadar remains strictly on your device. We have no means of accessing or viewing user-entered data.

Data Usage by RestRadar

Anonymized crash reports and device information are exclusively used to resolve app crashes and improve overall performance. Aggregated metrics, including total download counts, may be utilized for marketing purposes.

Your Consent

By using RestRadar, you consent to the practices outlined in this Privacy Policy.

Changes to this Policy

We may periodically update this Privacy Policy to ensure its accuracy and reflect any changes in our data practices. Any modifications will be posted on this page, so we encourage you to review it regularly. The "Last Updated" date at the top indicates the latest revision.
